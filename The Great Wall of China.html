The Great Wall of China

Home

China's Great Wall is known all over the world. This wall was built in many parts by the rulers there to protect their kingdom, which was gradually added, which has now become a castle shape. It was constructed from the 7th century to the 16th century. This great artwork is so strong, and huge that it was called the Great Wall of China. The vastness of this wall of China has also been seen by scientists from space, this man-made artwork is also visible from there. It is composed of soil, stone, brick, wood and other materials. This huge wall extends from Dandong to the east and extends to the west. China’s wall extends to approximately 6400 kilometers, and it is 35 feet high. This wall is like a fort; its width is so great that 10-15 people can walk comfortably in it.A 4,160-mile wall was built to protect China from invading Huns, Mongols, and other tribes, and to unite fortifications into one defense system. Begun in the 7th century B.C., the barricade took hundreds of years to build, and ranks as the world’s longest man-made structure and is the only one visible from space.

Facts

  • Contrary to popular belief the structure is not visible from outer space and is made up from several separate structures.
  • The wall was originally built to protect the Chinese states and empires from invaders and raids, but also acts as a useful boarder control.
