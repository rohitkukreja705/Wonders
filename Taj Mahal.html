The Taj Mahal of India is also one of the Seven Wonders of the World because of its beautiful artwork. The construction of the Taj Mahal was done by Shah Jahan built in 1630 by emperor, in honor of his dead wife, this white marble structure combines Indian, Persian, and Islamic style of architectureIt is a symbol of love, which Shah Jahan made to remember his wife Mamtaz. This mausoleum made of white marble with a garden around it. Tajmahal is located in Agra city of India. Beautiful artwork like this will not be seen in the world. When it was built by Mughal ruler Shah Jahan, it took 15 years, and after making it, the king had cut all the workers connected with the construction, so that they could not make such a second. In India, the Mughals ruled for a long time, during this time they had made many artifacts, artworks, which are present in India till date. To see the beauty of the Taj Mahal, people from the world come from far away.

Facts

  • Construction of the Taj Mahal started on 1631 shortly after the passing of the Emperor’s wife and took more than two decades at a cost of 32 million rupees (today’s equivalent of $827 million).
  • The Taj Mahal attracts 7–8 million visitors a year.
